# Power BI wireframe (HR demo)

This document provides page-level design guidance for a 3-page Power BI report that pairs well with the synthetic datasets generated by `generate_demo_data.py`.

## Page 1 — Executive overview

**Goal:** One-screen HR health snapshot with clear drivers.

**Recommended visuals**

- KPI cards
  - Headcount (active)
  - Avg performance rating (latest period)
  - Avg engagement score (latest survey)
  - Transfer rate (last 12 months)
- Trend lines
  - Avg rating over time (by review period)
  - Engagement over time (survey cadence)
- Breakdown
  - Headcount by department
  - Avg rating by department

**Interactions**

- Cross-filter by department and manager
- Date slicer (review period)

## Page 2 — Manager coaching impact

**Goal:** Showcase the causal rules: coaching_factor and manager bias influence ratings.

**Recommended visuals**

- Scatter plot: `coaching_factor` vs avg `rating` (by manager)
  - Size: manager headcount
  - Color: department
- Bar: top/bottom managers by avg rating (latest period)
- Decomposition tree: rating drivers
  - Department → Manager → New grad vs Non-new grad

**Callouts**

- Explain that manager-level `rating_bias` is injected (demo-only) and can be used for fairness discussions.

## Page 3 — Learning & mobility

**Goal:** Show how learning (LMS) and transfers relate to performance and sentiment.

**Recommended visuals**

- Histogram: LMS enrollments per employee (last 6 months)
- Line + markers: rating before/after transfer (transfer_recent flag)
- Matrix:
  - Rows: department
  - Columns: review period
  - Values: avg rating, avg LMS enrollments
- Optional: survey engagement by LMS activity tier

**Interactions**

- Employee drill-through page (optional)
  - Timeline: assignments, LMS enrollments, survey responses, performance reviews
